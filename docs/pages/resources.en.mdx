# Resources Overview
The following data resources are available through our API:
- [Books](/resources/books): A list of all books from the Harry Potter universe.
- [Chapters](/resources/chapters): A list of all chapters of each book from the Harry Potter universe.
- [Characters](/resources/characters): A list of all characters from the Harry Potter universe.
- [Movies](/resources/movies): A list of all movies from the Harry Potter universe.
- [Potions](/resources/potions): A list of all potions from the Harry Potter universe.
- [Spells](/resources/spells): A list of all spells from the Harry Potter universe.
